Webinar: Rev 3.0 – From Complex Configuration to Autonomous Revenue with Salesforce Agentforce Revenue Management
Mar 25, 2026  |  Virtual Event

Watch the on-demand recording of our webinar, where Salesforce and Forsys experts explore how modern enterprises are moving from complex revenue configurations to Autonomous Revenue — AI-driven, unified, and continuously optimized with Salesforce Agentforce Revenue Management.

Brion Schweers Former SVP of Revenue Cloud Solutions, Salesforce, traced the evolution from the ERP era (Rev 1.0) to CPQ (Rev 2.0) to today's AI-powered Rev 3.0 — making the case that organizations must modernize their revenue lifecycle now or risk falling behind.

Srinivas Vemuri, Co-Founder & COO, Forsys, shared how Agentforce Revenue Management simplifies complex configurations with a powerful out-of-the-box engine, enabling sales teams to leverage AI agents for smarter, more efficient revenue operations.

Raj Saladi, Director of Business Architecture, Forsys, brought the platform's capabilities to life through a live demo showcasing how Agentforce Revenue Management handles enterprise-scale configuration complexity.

Jan 21, 2026  |  Virtual Event

Watch the on-demand recording of our webinar, where revenue, product & business leaders explain why enterprises are moving beyond traditional CPQ to Agentforce Revenue Management.

A key highlight of the webinar was the Forsys RevRamp solution, designed to help enterprises seamlessly transition from CPQ to Agentforce Revenue Management. The session included insights from Muthu Murugan, Senior Director of Product Management at Salesforce, who shared perspectives on the evolution from CPQ to Agentforce Revenue Management and the platform capabilities enabling modern revenue organizations.

Bringing a real-world enterprise story to the forefront, our customer Smita Katariya, GVP – Business Applications at Colibri Group, shared her perspective on navigating revenue transformation within her organization.
