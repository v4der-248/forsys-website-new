The Challenges Hi-Tech Companies Face

Complex product catalogs, aggressive pricing cycles, and fragmented CRM data demand a purpose-built Lead-to-Revenue platform.

Hi-tech companies operate in one of the most demanding revenue environments — constantly evolving product lines, multi-tiered pricing structures, channel complexity, and the relentless pressure to accelerate quote-to-cash. Legacy CPQ tools and disconnected systems leave sales teams bogged down in manual processes while leadership lacks the real-time visibility needed to forecast accurately and act decisively.

  • Multi-cloud pricing complexity slowing time-to-quote
  • Revenue leakage from disconnected CPQ, CLM, and billing systems
  • Sales data silos preventing accurate forecasting
  • Rapid product innovation outpacing configure-price-quote workflows
