SaaS Landscape

Challenges SaaS Companies Must Overcome

Subscription billing complexity, revenue recognition under ASC 606, churn prevention, and multi-tier pricing models create unique Lead-to-Revenue challenges for SaaS and subscription businesses.

SaaS and subscription businesses operate in one of the most dynamic and unforgiving revenue environments — constantly evolving pricing tiers, usage-based billing models, and the relentless pressure of renewal cycles. Revenue recognition compliance under ASC 606 and IFRS 15 adds further complexity, while churn risk demands real-time customer health visibility that legacy systems simply cannot provide.

  • Complex subscription pricing tiers, add-ons, and usage-based billing models
  • Revenue recognition compliance under ASC 606 / IFRS 15 across contract modifications
  • Renewal management and churn prevention requiring real-time customer health signals
  • CPQ bottlenecks slowing deal velocity for rapidly evolving product catalogs
