How LexiShift Works

From unstructured PDFs to contract intelligence

LexiShift uses AI to read contracts the way a lawyer would — but at machine speed and enterprise scale.

01

Ingest

Connect LexiShift to your document repository — SharePoint, cloud storage, email, or direct upload — and watch it ingest thousands of contracts in minutes, regardless of format or vintage.

02

Extract

LexiShift’s AI reads each contract and extracts 50+ data elements — parties, dates, financial terms, obligations, termination clauses, and risk indicators — with human-reviewable confidence scores.

03

Export & Integrate

Structured contract data exports directly to Conga CLM, Salesforce, SharePoint, or your data warehouse — migration-ready, analytics-ready, and AI-ready.

Key Features

Purpose-built for enterprise contract extraction

50+ Data Elements

LexiShift extracts parties, effective dates, expiration dates, payment terms, renewal options, termination rights, SLA commitments, and 40+ additional contract fields from any contract type.

Multi-Format Support

Handles PDFs (native and scanned), Word documents, and older formats — with OCR for scanned legacy contracts and layout-aware extraction that handles complex table structures.

Confidence Scoring

Every extracted field comes with a confidence score — high-confidence fields are auto-approved; lower-confidence fields are flagged for human review — with a simple, fast review interface.

Conga CLM Integration

LexiShift connects directly to Conga CLM — pushing structured contract data into Conga’s contract record structure, populating all required fields for a clean, complete CLM migration.

Obligation & Renewal Tracking

LexiShift identifies contractual obligations, milestone dates, and renewal windows — and can push these directly into calendar systems or CLM platforms for automated alerting.

Batch Processing

Process thousands of contracts simultaneously using LexiShift’s cloud-native batch pipeline — with a real-time progress dashboard, error queue management, and extraction quality reports.
