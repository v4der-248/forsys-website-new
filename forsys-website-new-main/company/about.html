Who We Are

Focused on One Thing

Forsys is exclusively focused on Lead-to-Revenue transformation — from first lead to collected cash.

Forsys Inc. is a business consulting and technology services firm exclusively focused on Lead-to-Revenue transformation. We partner with enterprise clients across Hi-Tech, Manufacturing, Healthcare, Financial Services, SaaS, and Education to modernize their revenue operations using the best platforms in the market — Salesforce, Conga, Oracle, and our own AI-powered solutions.

Our team of 1,000+ Lead-to-Revenue specialists brings deep platform expertise, proven industry templates, and AI-powered accelerators that reduce implementation risk and accelerate time-to-value. With offices in Milpitas CA, Round Rock TX, and our 21,000 sq ft Hyderabad headquarters, Forsys operates as a global firm with local engagement teams.

Our Journey

Building a Revenue Transformation Company

  1. 2012
    Forsys Founded

    Forsys Inc. established in Milpitas, California — exclusively focused on Salesforce CRM and CPQ consulting for hi-tech enterprises.

  2. 2015
    Conga Partnership

    Forsys became a Conga Lead-to-Revenue Partner, expanding capabilities into CPQ, CLM, and Revenue Management.

  3. 2018
    Oracle Gold Partner

    Forsys earned Oracle Gold Partner and Cloud Excellence Implementation Partner status for Fusion Cloud SCM.

  4. 2019
    ISO 9001 Certification

    ISO 9001:2015 quality management certification achieved — formalizing Forsys's commitment to delivery excellence.

  5. 2021
    Salesforce Summit Partner

    Forsys reached Salesforce Summit Partner status — the highest partner tier — reflecting 200+ completed engagements and a 4.8/5 CSAT rating.

  6. 2022
    Great Place to Work

    First Great Place to Work certification in India — beginning what would become 6 consecutive years of GPTW recognition.

  7. 2024
    Hyderabad HQ Opens

    Forsys opened a 21,000 sq ft, 400-seater headquarters in Hyderabad's Financial District — representing a ₹17 crore investment and plans for 500+ hires by 2026.

  8. 2025
    AI-First Transformation

    Launched Agentforce Revenue Management practice, RevRamp, LexiShift, AITest, and the Forsys AI Solutions portfolio — becoming an AI-native Lead-to-Revenue firm.
