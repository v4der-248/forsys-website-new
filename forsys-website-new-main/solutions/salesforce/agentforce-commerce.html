Key Features

Commerce capabilities that drive revenue

Every feature we deploy is designed to reduce buying friction and accelerate revenue.

CPQ-Integrated Configuration

Native integration with Salesforce Revenue Management for real-time product configuration and accurate pricing within the commerce experience — no manual quote handoffs.

Omnichannel Order Management

Unified order capture across web, portal, mobile, and sales-assisted channels — with real-time ERP integration for accurate inventory and fulfillment visibility.

Einstein Recommendation Engine

Einstein-powered product recommendations and cross-sell/upsell suggestions that increase average order value — surfaced automatically within the buying journey.

Commerce Analytics

Real-time dashboards for conversion rates, average order value, channel mix, and customer lifetime value — giving RevOps visibility into commerce performance.

Mobile-First Architecture

Headless, API-first commerce architecture supporting high-volume B2B commerce on any device — with multi-currency, multi-region, and multi-language capabilities.

Managed Commerce Services

Post-launch commerce platform administration, catalog management, A/B testing, and continuous optimization — maximizing conversion and lifetime value.
