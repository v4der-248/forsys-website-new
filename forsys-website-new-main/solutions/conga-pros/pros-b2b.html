Key Features

Capabilities that accelerate B2B deal velocity

From AI-guided selling to approval automation — built for the full complexity of enterprise B2B quoting.

B2B Commerce Integration

PROS Smart CPQ powers both rep-assisted and self-service B2B buying — integrating with Conga Digital Commerce so quotes generated by reps can seamlessly convert to online orders with consistent pricing.

Intelligent Product Catalog

AI-managed product catalog that learns from historical sales to surface the most relevant product configurations, price points, and bundles for each customer segment and deal scenario.

Configuration Rules Engine

Advanced configuration rules handling complex product interdependencies, regional compliance constraints, and channel-specific option availability — eliminating invalid configurations and quote rework.

Quote Guidance & Win Scoring

For every quote, PROS AI surfaces a win-probability score, optimal price recommendation, and deal risk factors — giving sales reps and managers the intelligence to prioritize and close deals faster.

Approval Workflow Automation

Multi-tier approval workflows with automated routing based on discount levels, deal size, and margin thresholds — reducing approval cycle time while maintaining pricing governance.

Revenue Analytics

End-to-end revenue analytics from quote to close — tracking win rates, deal velocity, margin performance, and pricing effectiveness across reps, segments, and product lines.
