Privacy Policy

How Forsys Inc. collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our website and services.

This Privacy Policy covers forsysinc.com and related services. Forsys, Inc., a California corporation, is committed to protecting your privacy online. By accessing or using our site, you agree to be bound by all terms and conditions of this policy. Forsys reserves the right to modify this policy at any time, with changes taking effect upon notice via email or website posting.

1. What Information Do We Collect?

We collect two types of information from visitors to our site:

Personally Identifiable Information (PII) — collected when you register, order products or services, submit content, post in discussion forums, enter contests, complete surveys, or provide feedback. This may include:

  • First and last name
  • Mailing address and email address
  • Employer, job title, and department
  • Phone and fax numbers
  • Credit card numbers (for transactions)

Non-Personally Identifiable Information — collected automatically as you use the site, including the URL you came from, your browser type, IP address, and navigation patterns.

2. How Is Your Information Collected and Used?

Information is collected at various points including service registration, special offer sign-ups, email or form submissions, and credit card transactions.

Personally Identifiable Information is used to:

  • Provide contracted services and fill orders
  • Enhance site operations and improve marketing efforts
  • Analyze site usage statistics and customize content
  • Deliver administrative notices and resolve disputes
  • Enforce our agreements with you

Non-Personally Identifiable Information is used to diagnose server problems, administer the site, gather demographic data, and provide summary advertising reports.

3. Who Receives Your Information?

We do not sell, trade, or rent your Personally Identifiable Information to others.

We may share your information only with service partners under confidentiality agreements — including credit card processors, delivery services, and customer service providers. The use of your information by these partners is governed by their own privacy policies.

Forsys complies with all law enforcement requests, court orders, and subpoenas. We reserve the right to report unlawful activities to law enforcement. Non-personally identifiable information may be shared with third-party advertisers regarding sales and traffic patterns, without identifying individuals.

4. How Can You Update or Correct Your Information?

You can update your information through your online account or by contacting us at privacy@forsysinc.com (include your name, address, and email).

Please note that information associated with past transactions cannot be deleted. Backup systems may retain residual copies of your information.

5. Your Choices Regarding Data Collection

You can opt out of marketing email offers and direct mail at any time through your account settings.

By modifying your browser preferences, you can choose to accept all cookies, be notified when a cookie is set, or reject all cookies. Please note that rejecting all cookies may impair certain site functionality.

6. How We Protect Your Information

Your account is password-protected with an encrypted password, unique customer ID, and access limited to authorized personnel only.

Your Personally Identifiable Information is encrypted during transmission to prevent unauthorized viewing. Credit card and fulfillment processing is handled by established banking and distribution institutions.

However, no data transmission over the Internet or any wireless network can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. By using our site, you acknowledge the inherent security limitations of the Internet and that data may potentially be intercepted during transmission.

7. Cookies

Cookies are stored on your device to facilitate a customized site experience. Cookie data may include a randomly assigned user ID, your country, and first name.

Third-party advertisers may place separate cookies that collect non-personally identifiable data such as IP address, browser type, and zip code. No personally identifiable information is provided to ad servers.

8. Third-Party Site Privacy Policies

This Privacy Policy applies only to forsysinc.com. Other sites accessible through our site have their own privacy policies and data collection practices. Forsys is not responsible for the policies or practices of third parties.

9. Other Privacy Considerations

Age Requirement: You must be at least 18 years old to use this site. We do not knowingly collect, use, or disclose Personally Identifiable Information about visitors under 18 years of age.

Public Forums: Information shared in discussion forums or public areas of the site can be collected and used by third parties. Such activities are beyond Forsys's control.

User Responsibility: You are solely responsible for maintaining the secrecy of your password and account information.
