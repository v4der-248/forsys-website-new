Our Approach

A structured path from confusion to confidence

Our Build vs Buy engagements follow a proven six-step process refined across 200+ technology evaluations.

Define Requirements & Constraints

We work with your stakeholders to capture functional requirements, integration needs, budget parameters, and timeline expectations — the foundation of every evaluation.

  • Requirements workshops with key stakeholders
  • Current-state system and integration audit
  • Budget and timeline constraint definition
  • Success criteria and decision criteria agreement

Market Landscape Discovery

We map the vendor landscape and shortlist viable options across packaged SaaS, platform extensions, and custom build approaches relevant to your use case.

  • Vendor landscape mapping
  • Long-list development and initial scoring
  • Custom build feasibility assessment
  • Reference customer identification

Capability Benchmarking

Each shortlisted option is scored against your requirements across functional fit, integration capability, scalability, and vendor health — producing a weighted comparison matrix.

  • Detailed feature-fit scoring
  • Vendor demo facilitation
  • Integration and API capability assessment
  • Vendor financial and roadmap review

TCO & Financial Modeling

We build detailed three-to-five year TCO models for each option, covering all cost dimensions — from licensing and implementation through to ongoing support and upgrade cycles.

  • License and subscription cost modeling
  • Implementation and integration cost estimation
  • Training and change management costs
  • Ongoing maintenance and upgrade projections
  • NPV and payback period analysis

Recommendation & Decision Artifacts

We present findings in an executive-ready format — a clear recommendation backed by data, with the trade-off analysis and risk register needed for confident decision-making.

  • Executive recommendation with supporting analysis
  • Trade-off summary and risk register
  • Vendor negotiation guidance
  • Implementation readiness assessment

Implementation Transition

Once the decision is made, we support the transition to implementation — including vendor contracting guidance, implementation team selection, and project kick-off planning.

  • Contract and commercial negotiation support
  • Implementation partner selection
  • Implementation roadmap and governance
  • Knowledge transfer to implementation team
