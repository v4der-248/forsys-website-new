Our Approach

AI strategy to production in six structured phases

Built from Forsys's early-adopter experience with Agentforce and generative AI across 500+ revenue transformation engagements.

AI Readiness Assessment

We evaluate your current AI maturity across data quality, process automation potential, team capability, and technology readiness — establishing a scored baseline.

  • Data quality and governance audit
  • Process automation maturity scoring
  • Team AI capability assessment
  • Technology and integration readiness review

Use-Case Identification & Prioritization

We map all candidate AI use cases across your revenue operations and score each on business impact, data availability, process complexity, and implementation feasibility.

  • Revenue workflow AI opportunity mapping
  • Impact vs feasibility scoring matrix
  • Quick-win identification for 90-day pilots
  • Strategic use-case backlog for long-term roadmap

Agentforce Agent Architecture Design

We design the agent topology — defining which agents handle which tasks, what data they access, how they escalate, and how they integrate with your CRM and revenue platforms.

  • Agent role and responsibility definition
  • Data access and privacy design
  • Escalation and human-in-the-loop design
  • Integration architecture with Salesforce and revenue systems

Pilot Deployment

We deploy a focused pilot with one to two high-priority use cases — measuring performance, refining agent behavior, and establishing the success metrics for full-scale deployment.

  • Pilot scope and success criteria definition
  • Agent deployment and configuration
  • Performance monitoring and feedback loops
  • Pilot results analysis and readout

Full-Scale Deployment

We scale from pilot to production — deploying agents across the prioritized use-case backlog, integrating with live data, and training the teams who will manage them.

  • Production deployment and integration
  • Agent training and performance tuning
  • User enablement and adoption programs
  • Governance model activation

Continuous Optimization & Scale

We establish an AI operations rhythm — monitoring agent performance, expanding use cases, and continuously improving agent accuracy and business impact.

  • Agent performance monitoring and reporting
  • Continuous improvement and retraining
  • New use-case identification and onboarding
  • AI governance reviews and compliance audits
