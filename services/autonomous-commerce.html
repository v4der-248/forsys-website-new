Our Approach

From fragmented channels to frictionless revenue

Our Autonomous Commerce engagements follow a proven process refined across 25+ digital commerce implementations.

Buying Journey Discovery

We map the current buying journey end to end — from initial interest through configuration, pricing, order placement, and fulfillment — identifying every friction point and drop-off.

  • Buyer journey mapping and friction analysis
  • Channel coverage audit (self-service, assisted, partner)
  • Current platform and integration assessment
  • Revenue opportunity sizing from reduced friction

Commerce Architecture Blueprint

We design the target-state commerce architecture — defining channels, platform components, integration points, and the agentic journey overlay that removes friction from the buying process.

  • Channel strategy and prioritization
  • Commerce platform selection (Conga, Agentforce, RISE)
  • Integration architecture with CPQ, ERP, and billing
  • Agentic journey architecture and AI touchpoint design

UX and Journey Design

We design the user experience for every buyer persona — from a B2B procurement manager configuring a complex product bundle to a reseller placing a repeat order through a partner portal.

  • Buyer persona definition and journey mapping
  • Self-service portal UX wireframes
  • Configuration and pricing flow design
  • Mobile and responsive design specifications

Platform Selection & Business Case

We run a structured platform evaluation across Conga Digital Commerce, Agentforce Commerce, PROS Smart CPQ, and custom build — delivering a TCO-backed recommendation with an implementation business case.

  • Platform capability benchmarking
  • TCO and NPV modeling
  • Implementation risk and timeline analysis
  • Executive-ready recommendation artifacts

Commerce Build & Integration

We implement the commerce platform — configuring product catalogs, pricing rules, configuration logic, and buyer workflows — integrated with your ERP, billing, and payment systems.

  • Platform configuration and customization
  • Product catalog and pricing setup
  • Integration with ERP, billing, and payment gateways
  • Performance and security testing
  • User acceptance testing and go-live support

Continuous Optimization

Post-launch, we monitor commerce performance, analyze buyer behavior, and continuously optimize the buying journey — expanding channels, adding AI capabilities, and improving conversion.

  • Commerce analytics and reporting
  • Buyer behavior analysis and A/B testing
  • AI agent performance tuning
  • Channel expansion and new feature rollout
  • Managed services for ongoing commerce operations
