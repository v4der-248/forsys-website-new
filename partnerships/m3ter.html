About m3ter

Metered Billing Infrastructure for Modern SaaS

m3ter is a usage-based billing and monetization platform purpose-built for software companies. It provides the infrastructure to meter any usage event, apply complex pricing rules, and automate invoicing — enabling companies to move from seat-based to consumption-based models without re-architecting their billing stack.

  • Real-time usage metering at any scale
  • Flexible pricing: per-seat, consumption, hybrid, tiered
  • Automated invoice generation and revenue recognition
  • Native integrations with Salesforce, Stripe, NetSuite, and more
  • Analytics on usage trends, pricing performance, and revenue forecasting

How m3ter Extends Forsys Revenue Solutions
