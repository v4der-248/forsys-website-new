About Rocketlane

Professional Services Automation for Revenue Teams

Rocketlane is a purpose-built Professional Services Automation (PSA) platform that transforms how services teams plan, execute, and track client onboarding and implementation projects. With real-time collaboration portals, resource management, and project analytics, Rocketlane gives both service teams and clients unprecedented visibility.

  • Client onboarding collaboration portals with real-time status
  • Resource capacity planning and utilization tracking
  • Project health dashboards and milestone tracking
  • Time-tracking, billing integration, and revenue recognition
  • Automated project templates for faster kickoffs
