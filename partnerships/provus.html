About Provus

AI-Driven Services CPQ for Enterprise Teams

Provus is an AI-powered Services CPQ (Configure Price Quote) platform purpose-built for professional services organizations. Unlike traditional product CPQ tools, Provus understands services scope, resource requirements, and pricing dynamics — generating accurate, margin-aware quotes for complex service engagements in minutes instead of days.

  • AI-powered scope and effort estimation
  • Resource-based pricing and margin optimization
  • Integration with Salesforce CRM for seamless quoting
  • Real-time resource availability and capacity checks
  • Analytics on win rates, pricing, and margin by service type

How Provus Enhances Forsys Engagements
