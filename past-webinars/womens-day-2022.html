Happy International Women’s Day!!

March 8, 2022  |  Live Event

“A strong woman knows she has strength enough for the journey, but a woman of strength knows that it is in the journey where she will become strong.”

— Luke Easter

Wishing everyone a Happy International Women’s Day!

As the theme for International Women’s Day 2022 focuses on breaking the bias, together we can forge women’s equality and create a more inclusive and gender-equal world when we actively call out gender bias, discrimination and stereotyping every time we encounter it.

We understand that as much as breaking the bias needs conviction and courage, it also needs a deeply undervalued ability to respect others’ interests while making choices. Women deserve and want a future free from stigma, stereotypes and bias; a future that’s peaceful, sustainable, with equal rights and opportunities. And to get us there, women are needed at every table where decisions are being made.

We are proud of the strong and influential women we have here at Forsys who all contribute to our company’s success. You have filled our workplace and this professional environment with your constant love, care, nurture, support and compassion.
